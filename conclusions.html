Conclusions

  • During the Geovisualization course, I gained a solid understanding of web development theory alongside practical skills in managing and displaying geographical data through QGIS and ArcGIS Pro.

  • The course provided complete documentation, and the instructions for the exercises were clear enough for us students to follow easily. The tasks we had to do ranged from simple to more complex ones, which meant we had to manage our time well to finish our portfolios by the deadline.

  • However, I faced one challenge - the course was taught entirely in German. Even though the materials were in English, it sometimes became tough for me and other international students who do not speak German to keep up with the lectures.
