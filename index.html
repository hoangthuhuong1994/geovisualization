Portfolio Introduction

Objective and Motivation of the Portfolio

This portfolio was developed during the Geovisualisation module in the M.sc. Geoinformatics program in the winter term of 2023/24. It encompasses the various topics covered during the course.

The Geovisualisation module aims to provide a practical understanding of Geographical Information Systems (GIS) alongside theoretical concepts and showcase the practical application of GIS tools and techniques learned during the course.

The content of this portfolio includes

  • Introduction to Web-Development.
  • Introduction to GIS.
  • Theory of Geovisualization.
  • Analyzing Flood Risk Mapping in Trier.
  • Revised Universal Soil Loss Equation (RUSLE) project in West-Kenia.
  • Conclusions.
